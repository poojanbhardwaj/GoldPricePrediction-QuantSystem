# Screenshot Checklist

Reviewed deployment screenshots can be added under `docs/screenshots/` without changing application behavior.

Recommended captures:

- `public_preview.png` - public Research Dashboard with source-labeled market cards
- `login.png` - sign-in, account creation, and verification/local-development notice
- `watchlist.png` - Candidate Watchlist rankings and blockers
- `evidence.png` - Evidence & Validation metrics and benchmark comparison
- `research_history.png` - latest-versus-previous saved research changes
- `account_settings.png` - account mode and research-only preferences

Before capturing, confirm that no email address, secret, private database path, or user export is visible. Saved or cached prices must retain their provenance and freshness labels.
