# Recruiter Demo Script

## 1. Public Preview

Open the deployed app as a signed-out visitor. Point out the multi-asset market snapshot, source/freshness labels, methodology preview, and explicit research-only boundaries. Saved and cached values are visible but are never described as live.

## 2. Account Access

Open **Login / Sign Up**. In a Supabase deployment, explain that email ownership must be verified before workspace access. In a local run, show the visible Local development warning and explain that passwords are salted hashes but email ownership is not verified.

## 3. User Goals

Open **User Goals & Saved Plans**. Select experience, goal, risk comfort, preferred assets, horizon, and explanation style. Generate a personalized plan and show that uncertain or blocked evidence stays visible.

## 4. Candidate Watchlist

Open **Candidate Watchlist**. Review how all supported assets are ranked from the latest labeled snapshot. Emphasize that classifications are research priorities, not financial recommendations.

## 5. Evidence of Edge

Open **Evidence of Edge**. Compare active estimates with passive baselines, costs, validation metrics, and evidence grades. Show rejected rows as proof that the platform does not hide weak candidates.

## 6. Research History

Open **Research History & Changes** after saving two plan runs. Review upgrades, downgrades, stable assets, and the latest-vs-previous table. Explain that history is user-owned and compares immutable saved evidence rather than recalculating the past.

## 7. Account Settings

Open **Account & Settings**. Show provider/verification mode, saved-run counts, and harmless research preferences. Note the explicit absence of broker, bank, execution-key, or real-money settings.

## Close

Summarize the engineering story: a modular forecasting and validation pipeline, conservative risk and benchmark controls, an auth-ready user platform, immutable research history, honest provenance, and automated regression coverage. The system is research-only and does not execute trades.
